# AI Recovery Companion - Feature Vision

> **The Core Idea:** An intelligent, personal AI companion that knows your entire recovery journey, guides you through the 12 steps, and is there 24/7 when humans can't be.

## 🎯 Two Pillars

### 1. AI Recovery Companion
Your personal support system that:
- Remembers everything you share (journals, chats, moods)
- Learns your patterns, triggers, and what helps
- Available 24/7 for support, venting, processing
- References YOUR history, not generic advice
- Speaks recovery language (Big Book, 12-step principles)

### 2. 12-Step Process Guide
Intelligent step work assistance:
- Guides you through each step at YOUR pace
- Interactive worksheets (4th step inventory, 8th step list)
- Knows where you are in your journey
- Suggests when you might be ready for next step
- Connects step work to your personal experiences

### Intelligence Layers

1. **Memory Extraction**
   - Every journal entry → extract facts, emotions, patterns
   - Every chat → update understanding of user
   - Background pattern detection

2. **Context Assembly**
   - When user chats → pull relevant memories
   - Build personalized context for AI
   - Include recovery knowledge

3. **Proactive Outreach**
   - Detect concerning patterns
   - Check-in at strategic times
   - Celebrate milestones
   - Gentle nudges when engagement drops

4. **Step Work Intelligence**
   - Track progress through steps
   - Generate personalized prompts
   - Connect past shares to current work
   - Suggest readiness for next step

## 💬 Conversation Examples

### Deep Personal Support
```
User: "I can't stop thinking about my ex. I know I should focus 
       on recovery but she's all I think about."

AI: "That's really hard. Relationships and recovery are deeply 
     connected - you mentioned in your 4th step work that fear 
     of abandonment is one of your core fears. 
     
     When you were with her, did drinking help you avoid those 
     feelings? Sometimes understanding that connection helps us 
     see the bigger picture.
     
     Have you talked to Mike [sponsor] about this? You told me 
     last month that he went through something similar."
```

### Step Work Guidance
```
User: "I think I'm ready to start Step 4"

AI: "That's a big step - I'm glad you feel ready. You've been 
     sober 47 days now and you've been working a solid program.
     
     Step 4 is about a 'searching and fearless moral inventory.' 
     Before we dive in, I want you to know this can bring up 
     hard feelings. Make sure you have support in place.
     
     When's a good time to schedule regular check-ins with Mike 
     during this work?
     
     When you're ready, we can start with resentments. Want me 
     to explain the four-column inventory method, or do you 
     already know it from your home group?"
```

### Crisis Support
```
User: "I bought a bottle. It's sitting on my counter."

AI: "I'm here. You haven't opened it yet - that matters.
     
     Can you do one thing for me right now? Put distance between 
     you and that bottle. Another room, outside, anywhere.
     
     Then let's talk, or call Mike. His number is [tap to call].
     
     You've made it 47 days. You told me on Day 7 that if you 
     could just make it one month, you'd prove to yourself it 
     was possible. You did that. You've already proven it.
     
     What's going on tonight? I'm not going anywhere."
```

## 📋 12-Step Process Helper

### For Each Step

**Step 1: Powerlessness**
- Guided reflection prompts
- "Write about a time your addiction made your life unmanageable"
- Track when user genuinely accepts vs. intellectual understanding

**Step 2: Came to Believe**
- Explore higher power concept
- No religious pressure - "power greater than yourself"
- Save their personal definition

**Step 3: Decision**
- Guided through the decision
- Third step prayer (if they want)
- Mark the date

**Step 4: Moral Inventory**
- Interactive resentment worksheet
- Fear inventory
- Sex conduct review
- Guides through Big Book method
- All encrypted, deeply private

**Step 5: Admission**
- Prepare for sharing with sponsor/clergy
- Checklist of what to share
- Post-step reflection

**Step 6: Ready for Removal**
- Character defects identification
- Willingness assessment
- "Are you ready to have these removed?"

**Step 7: Humbly Asked**
- Seventh step prayer
- Ongoing awareness tracker
- "Did pride show up today?"

**Step 8: Made a List**
- Interactive amends list builder
- Categorize: direct, living, impossible
- Willingness to make each amends

**Step 9: Made Amends**
- Track amends progress
- Plan each conversation
- Record outcomes
- Living amends commitments

**Step 10: Daily Inventory**
- Evening review prompts
- Quick daily check-in
- Pattern tracking

**Step 11: Prayer & Meditation**
- Morning routine builder
- Meditation timer
- Evening reflection
- Spiritual fitness tracking

**Step 12: Carrying the Message**
- Service opportunities
- Sponsee tracking (if sponsoring)
- How you're giving back

## 🔐 Privacy Principles

1. **All data encrypted** - AES-256, only user can decrypt
2. **Memory stays on device** - Never uploaded unencrypted
3. **AI calls are transient** - No server-side storage
4. **User owns their data** - Export anytime, delete anytime
5. **Sponsor access is opt-in** - User controls exactly what's shared
