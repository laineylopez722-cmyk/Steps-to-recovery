**Full Description:**

Steps to Recovery is the first 12-step app that actually helps you work the steps—not just count days.

🤖 **AI Companion That Knows You**
Unlike generic chatbots, our AI remembers your story. Your triggers, your wins, your patterns. It's like having a sponsor in your pocket—available at 2am when you need it most.

- Journal with mood tracking
